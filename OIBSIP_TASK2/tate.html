Biography

Andrew Tate was born on December 18, 1986, in Chicago, Illinois. He is a four-time kickboxing world champion and has competed in multiple other combat sports, including MMA and boxing. Tate is also known for his controversial comments on social media and his appearances on reality TV shows like "Celebrity Big Brother" and "Ex on the Beach."

Despite his controversial persona, Tate is widely respected in the combat sports world for his impressive record and skill as a fighter.

Accomplishments

  • Four-time World Kickboxing Association (WKA) World Champion
  • Two-time International Kickboxing Federation (IKF) World Champion
  • One-time Enfusion Kickboxing World Champion
  • One-time ISKA World Cruiserweight Champion
  • 26-0 Kickboxing Record
